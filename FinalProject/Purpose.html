Why I Am Creating This Website

First off, I would like to welcome you to my Personal Goals Website! I have created this website as a public representation of my goals and things that I plan on getting done throughout the year of 2018. I actually got the idea from a YouTuber called Thomas Frank (go check him out!).




The theory behind making a website like this is so that people who view this website (close friends and family,etc.) can be able to hold you accountable to your goals so that you can take them more seriously (it's a psychological thing). Also, while creating a website like this, you are forcing yourself to think about and write down your goals as well as other things you may want to accomplish by the end of the year. This website also serves as a project for me to practice my web design skills by testing me in different areas of HTML/CSS.




The color scheme of this website uses some of the "Jewelry Stones" shades on the W3Schools website. Being that I have been looking into crystals and how they work (and if they work) lately, I was inspired to try to create a unique color scheme using the colors of certain crystals such as Peridot, Rose Quartz, Jasper, Moonstone, Amethyst, and Emerald. Being that I was born in the Month of May, Emerald is my birth stone, which is why it is primarily used throughout the wesite.
